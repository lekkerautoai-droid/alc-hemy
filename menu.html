Signature Cocktails

Original Concoctions

All cocktails are handcrafted with house-made syrups, infusions, and bitters. Please advise of any allergies.

✦ ✦ ✦
01
Ember & Oak
Bourbon · Smoked Maple · Walnut Bitters · Orange Peel

Charred oak staves, a veil of cold smoke, and the warmth of barrel-aged bourbon that lingers like an autumn fire. Served over a single hand-cut ice block.

R175
02
The Herbalist
Rye · House Chartreuse · Wild Thyme · Lemon Verbena

An apothecary's dream — garden-fresh herbs press against aged rye, balanced by a house-made green chartreuse and a float of cold-pressed verbena oil.

R155
03
Copper Solstice
Mezcal · Bitter Orange · Copper Bitters · Sal de Gusano

Mezcal's wild smoke meets sun-dried citrus and a hand-poured copper bitters reduction. Finished with a rim of artisan worm salt. Raw. Honest. Unforgettable.

R145
04
The Transmutation
Aquavit · Caraway Tincture · House Vermouth · Dill Flower

Scandinavian spirit meets the old apothecary. Caraway and fennel drift through a pale house vermouth, crowned with a single dill flower preserved in aspic.

R165
05
Midnight Tincture
Dark Rum · Activated Charcoal · Blackberry · Clove Smoke

Black as the witching hour. Aged rum is charcoal-filtered in-house, blended with late-harvest blackberry reduction and smoked over a sprig of clove. Served in a matte black coupe.

R185
06
The Alembic
Cognac · Saffron Honey · Cardamom · Rose Water

Named for the distillation vessel of the ancients. Aged cognac, elevated by a golden saffron honey reduction and a whisper of cardamom, finished with a mist of rose water.

R215
07
Golden Ratio
Calvados · Turmeric Honey · Lemon · Ginger Beer

The mathematics of pleasure. Apple brandy carries turmeric-infused honey into a long finish of preserved lemon and ginger beer. A formula we return to each season.

R145
08
The Forager's Dram
Scotch · Elderflower · Heather Honey · Pine Needle

Walk the Scottish moor without leaving your seat. Highland malt carries elderflower and heather honey to a finish as clean and wild as rain on bracken.

R205

01
Autumnal Elixir
Armagnac · Fig Leaf · Burnt Caramel · Mace

End-of-season figs slow-cooked into a syrup that carries the memory of summer. Armagnac adds leather and time. Mace adds the feeling of a warm kitchen at dusk.

02
The Distiller's Wife
Pear Eau de Vie · White Tea · Yuzu · Champagne Float

Delicate as the first frost. Pear eau de vie is steeped overnight in white tea, brightened with yuzu, and finished with a float of Champagne. Fragrant, precise, quietly devastating.

R195
03
Blossom & Bone
Mezcal · Cherry Blossom Syrup · Fennel Pollen · Salt

Spring's fleeting beauty expressed in a glass. Smoky mezcal meets a cherry blossom syrup made from fresh petals gathered each March. Fennel pollen and finishing salt.

R165
04
The Cartographer's Gin
Sloe Gin · Elderflower Cordial · Cucumber · Tonic

Maps old roads through English hedgerows. Sloe gin deepened with elder, laid over cucumber ribbons, and lengthened with house-made tonic water charged in-house.
