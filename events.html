Private Hire

Your evening, entirely.

ALC_Hemy is available for exclusive private hire — birthdays, anniversaries, corporate retreats, and the kind of celebrations that demand a setting as considered as the people in the room.

We close the bar to the public. We design a bespoke menu around your tastes. Our alchemists are your alchemists for the evening. Nothing is off the shelf. Everything is made for you.

Capacity: 8 to 60 guests · Available Tuesday through Sunday · Minimum spend applies.
